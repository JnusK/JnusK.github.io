BioFugal

Biofilm flow cell

Project Description

In this project, our team designed BioFugal, a biofilm flow cell that acts as a container to grow fungus in fluid. Biofulgal utilises centrifugal force to prevent growth of fungus at unwanted locations.

Research

We conducted primary and secondary research to determine the required specifications (minimum flow speed) of the biofilm as well as user preference (large viewing area).

Design

Our inspiration came from observing the flow of water accelerating at the bottom of the sink.

We designed with the user in mind with features such as a wide glass top for easy viewing of biofilm.

CAD

I learnt Solidworks to use it to render the flow cell. The flow was simulated using Solidworks Flow Simulation.
