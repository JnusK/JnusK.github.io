CoT

Designing the camping experience

Project Description

CoT was designed as a project in Introduction to Industrial Design course. I had to re-design the camping experience. I chose to work on a foldable cot that packs into a convenient box.

Ideate

I listed down the issues I had with camping and I chose to focus on the uncomfortable sleeping conditions. I sketched a few low-fidelity prototypes to explore the different options to sleep comfortably in the outdoors. I picked the foldable cot option as it was the most interesting design.

Functionalities

After narrowing down my options, I fleshed out the functionalities and features of the cot in greater detail. I tried both buttons and Velcro to fasten the cloth and after sketching them out, I preferred the aesthetics of the buttons.

Final Design

The final design was sketched and colored with descriptions to explain the design.
