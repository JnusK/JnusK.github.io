JuraSSiC

Autonomous evasive robot

Project Description

Design Competition requires participants to build a robot smaller than 9” diameter and 6” height that is programmed to detect and evade a pursuer autonomously while carrying a cup.

Research

We were given a Teensy microcontroller and an Xbee which will recieve the location of the pursuing robot.

We chose a tracked chassis as the base of our robot to achieve a small turning radius and stable base over uneven terrain.

We chose to use 3 different sensors for detection of different range and surfaces.

  • Button for sensing collision

  • Vive sensor for position and location of pursuing robot

  • Ultrasound sensor for obstacle detection

Algorithm

Our algorithm splits up the arena into quardrants and once our robot detects the pursuing robot, it will go to the furthest corner from the pursuing robot by hugging the walls.

We chose to use interrupts instead of polling for our bumpers to not waste processor cycles on polling for state of button.
